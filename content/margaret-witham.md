Margaret Witham

A life long communist, Margaret worked in the public sector for many years and was at one time a member of the executive of the Civil and Public Services Association (later merged into PCS).

She was also President of the Westminster Trades Council and the Greater London Pensioners Association for many years.

_Source: Morning Star 11th May 2000_
